Guidelines from the Department of Agriculture and the Department of Health and Human Services indicate that at least 30 minutes of daily physical activity is required to reduce the risk of chronic diseases. For some people it's more than 30 mins depending on other health conditions.


In the graph below, you can see the time spent by an average American on sports and physical activities.
